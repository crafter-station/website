Interview

María discusses her journey from working in traditional finance to founding her own fintech platform. She explains how witnessing the lack of accessible financial tools for everyday Peruvians inspired her to build a solution that combines education with practical financial management.

Throughout the conversation, María shares insights on building trust with users, the technical challenges of creating a secure financial platform, and the importance of regulatory compliance in fintech. She also talks about her vision for expanding financial inclusion across Latin America.

"Financial empowerment is about more than just tools—it's about education, trust, and creating systems that truly serve people's needs."
