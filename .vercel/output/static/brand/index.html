Naming

Always "Crafter Station" — two words, both capitalized. Never "CrafterStation", "crafter station", or abbreviated as "CS".

Usage guidelines

[*]

Use the white logo on dark backgrounds. Use the black logo on light backgrounds.

[*]

Do not recolor the logo. Do not apply gradients, shadows, or effects.

[*]

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "C" in the wordmark.

[*]

Do not stretch, rotate, or distort the logo in any direction.

[*]
